We are excited to announce that ReARM will be presented at the [CIS Summit](https://cis-events.com/) in Ottawa!

We will be welcoming guests in the **Canadian Pavilion**. Additionally, Pavel Shukhman, CEO of Reliza, will pitch ReARM during the **Wolf's Den Pitch Fest at 15:30 on the first day of the event (May 5)**. He will discuss Agentic AI development and why supply chain evidence platforms are essential to keep pace with the order-of-magnitude increase in code production speed that AI enables.

The event will be held on May 5 and 6, 2026 at Ottawa Conference and Event Centre, 200 Coventry Road, Ottawa, Ontario, K1K 4S3.
